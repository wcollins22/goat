Theodore Roosevelt

Often known as Teddy, Theodore Roosevelt is truly one of the great american icons. He is known for his overall bullish character which created many amazing historical moments.

The Rough Riders

This is him while fighting in Cuba. He fought in a calvary regiment nicknamed the "Rough Riders", because of their displayed bravery.

Teddy Bears

The name for stuffed bears, "Teddy Bear", comes from a famous story where Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear that his assistant tied to a tree.
